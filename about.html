About WinInstall Pro

WinInstall Pro is a free, student-friendly resource for anyone who wants to install Windows using a USB drive and Rufus. Our mission is to make the process simple, beautiful, and accessible for everyone, from beginners to techies.

This site is open source and maintained by students and volunteers. We believe in empowering people with clear, modern guides and stunning visuals.

Why trust us? Our guides are based on real-world experience, tested on dozens of devices, and updated regularly.
